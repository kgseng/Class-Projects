Maplestory offers a wide variety of classes to choose from!

From warriors who fight on the front lines, to thieves who fight
in the shadows, see for yourself what kind of playstyle suits you!


  • Warriors fight on the front lines, utilizing an arsenal of weapons to fight their foes.


  • Magicians fight from afar, utilizing spells and magic to defeat enemies.


  • Bowmen strike from a distance, identifying and attacking an enemy's weak points.


  • Thieves burst from the shadows, dealing large amounts of damage in a short amount of time.


  • Pirates blaze into battle with a flurry of attacks, one after another.
